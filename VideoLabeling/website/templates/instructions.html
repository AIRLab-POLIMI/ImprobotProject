Instructions

This experiment consists of a video with two interactive buttons (LIKE and DISLIKE).

You will be asked to watch the video and, in the meanwhile, express your appreciation by clicking on the rating button. The video consists of a series of improvised scenes between a robot and a human actor.

The scope of the experiment is to get when a particular scene is appreciated, or not, by the audience.

Please, try to be as reactive as possible by clicking on the relative button when you like or dislike a scene.
