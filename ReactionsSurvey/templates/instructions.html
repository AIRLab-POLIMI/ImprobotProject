Instructions

In this experiment, you will be asked to watch a series of video and, answer a question per each of these. This survey is about a robot actor, which is learning to improvise on a stage with an actor. In a video you can see one of fourteen scenic actions the robot can perform.

The scope of the experiment is to understand if people can recognize what a robot is trying to communicate.
